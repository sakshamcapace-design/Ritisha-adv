About the Advocate

Ritisha Mathur is a dedicated advocate based in New Delhi, specializing in delivering high-quality, bespoke legal solutions. Combining deep expertise in Indian law with a practical understanding of corporate dynamics, she prioritizes technical and structured strategic options.

Drawing inspiration from a philosophy of precision, Ritisha's practice handles complex regulatory environments, high-stakes dispute resolutions, and corporate legal restructuring.

Operating primarily out of Delhi, she routinely appears before the Supreme Court of India, the Delhi High Court, and the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT).
